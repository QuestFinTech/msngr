Outbound Queue

The outbound queue shows all messages that agents have requested to send. Messages go through a lifecycle before delivery:

Draft → Queued (passed policy check) → Sending → Sent or Failed

If a policy rule triggers a hold, the message goes to the Holds page for review. You can cancel queued messages before they're sent.
